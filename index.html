Prosody-Tacotron [1]: A state-of-the-art extension to Tacotron that synthesizes speech to match the prosody of referenace audio.
VITS (Emotion Label) : VITS with additional emotion label embedding for global conditioning
VITS (Spectrogram) : VITS with addition spectrogram embedding for global conditioning
IP-VITS (proposed) : The proposed end-to-end speech synthesis architecture that synthesizes emotional speech by explicit conditioning on the intensity and pitch of reference audio
